# CV Physical Demo Checklist

- Webcam or phone camera
- Stable mount or tripod
- 10-20 empty cardboard boxes
- Printed QR labels
- Normal, crushed, torn, dented, and open demo boxes
- Two vehicle/loading-bay markers
- Delivery bag or cargo container
- Hub board with colored tape zones
- Zone labels: INBOUND, QUEUE, SORTING, LOADING, OUTBOUND
- Backup prerecorded videos
- Good lighting and uncluttered background
